Veterans' Charities Say Trump Hasn't Handed Out Donations

Almost $4 million raised by Donald Trump's campaign at a veterans' charity fundraising event in January has yet to be distributed to the intended recipients, according to the Wall Street Journal. The newspaper reported that 19 of the 22 veterans' charities to which Trump promised to donate the proceeds, and found that only about $2.4 million of the $6 million raised has been distributed.
